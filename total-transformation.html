Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for?

The Total Transformation program is designed for anyone ready to make a significant, lasting change in their health and lifestyle. Whether you're dealing with chronic fatigue, weight issues, hormonal imbalances, or simply want to optimize your health, this program is tailored to meet you where you are and take you where you want to be.

Do I need medical clearance?

If you have any pre-existing chronic diseases or conditions, we recommend consulting with your physician before starting the program. Our comprehensive intake process will identify any potential contraindications, and we'll work with your healthcare team to ensure your safety throughout the transformation.

How much time does it require?

The program is designed to integrate seamlessly into your life. You'll need about 30-45 minutes daily for workouts, plus time for meal prep. Weekly check-ins and therapy sessions typically take 1-2 hours. We provide time-saving strategies and can adjust the program to fit your schedule.

What makes this different from other programs?

Unlike one-size-fits-all programs, every aspect of the Total Transformation is personalized to your unique biochemistry, health history, and goals. We use advanced diagnostics, provide in-office therapies, and offer weekly expert support to ensure your success. This isn't just a diet or workout plan – it's a complete health optimization system.

What happens after 12 weeks?

By week 12, you'll have established sustainable habits and a deep understanding of what your body needs to thrive. We provide a maintenance plan and ongoing support options to help you maintain and continue building on your transformation. Many clients choose to continue with monthly check-ins or seasonal tune-ups.
