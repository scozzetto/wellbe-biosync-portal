What are Vitamin Injections?

Vitamin injections are concentrated doses of essential vitamins and nutrients delivered directly into your muscle tissue for rapid absorption. This method bypasses the digestive system, ensuring maximum bioavailability and immediate benefits. Our most popular injections include B12, B-complex, vitamin D, and lipotropic blends for targeted health support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vitamin injections do you offer?

We offer several types of vitamin injections including B12 for energy and neurological health, B-complex for overall wellness, vitamin D for immune support and bone health, lipotropic injections for metabolism and weight management, and custom blends based on your specific nutritional needs and lab results.

Do vitamin injections hurt?

Most people experience only minor discomfort, similar to any intramuscular injection. The injection is typically given in the upper arm or buttock muscle and takes just a few seconds. Any soreness at the injection site is usually mild and resolves within a day or two.

How often should I get vitamin injections?

The frequency depends on your individual needs and the type of injection. B12 injections are often given weekly or bi-weekly initially, then monthly for maintenance. Vitamin D may be given monthly or quarterly based on your blood levels. We'll create a personalized schedule based on your lab results and health goals.
