What is Peptide Therapy?

Peptide therapy uses short chains of amino acids that naturally occur in the body to target specific biological pathways. These powerful molecules act as messengers, instructing cells to perform specific functions like healing, growth, immune response, and metabolism. Our peptide protocols are customized based on your individual needs and health goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What peptides do you offer?

We offer a range of therapeutic peptides including BPC-157 for healing and gut health, CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin for growth hormone support, Thymosin Alpha-1 for immune function, PT-141 for sexual health, and many others. Each peptide is selected based on your specific health goals and comprehensive laboratory assessment.

How are peptides administered?

Peptides can be administered through subcutaneous injections (most common), oral capsules, nasal sprays, or topical creams, depending on the specific peptide and your preferences. Most patients find subcutaneous injections easy to self-administer at home after proper training from our medical team.

When will I see results from peptide therapy?

Results vary by individual and peptide type. Some people notice improvements in energy and sleep within the first week, while others may take 4-6 weeks to see significant changes. Growth hormone-related peptides typically show benefits in body composition and recovery after 8-12 weeks of consistent use. We monitor your progress with regular follow-ups and lab work.
