What is NAD+ IV Infusion?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a vital coenzyme found in every cell of your body that powers cellular energy production and DNA repair. As we age, NAD+ levels decline dramatically, leading to decreased energy, cognitive decline, and accelerated aging. Our NAD+ IV infusion therapy delivers this essential molecule directly to your cells, bypassing the digestive system for maximum absorption and immediate benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a NAD+ IV infusion take?

NAD+ IV infusions typically take 2-4 hours depending on the dosage and your individual tolerance. The infusion must be administered slowly to prevent side effects like nausea or flushing. We provide a comfortable private room where you can relax, read, or work during your treatment.

How often should I get NAD+ IV therapy?

Treatment frequency depends on your individual needs and goals. For general wellness and anti-aging, monthly treatments are common. For specific conditions like addiction recovery or chronic fatigue, we may recommend more frequent sessions initially (2-3 times per week) followed by maintenance treatments. Your provider will create a personalized treatment plan based on your assessment.

Are there any side effects?

NAD+ IV therapy is generally well-tolerated, but some patients may experience mild side effects during infusion such as nausea, cramping, or flushing. These effects are typically temporary and can be minimized by adjusting the infusion rate. Our medical team monitors you throughout the treatment to ensure your comfort and safety.
