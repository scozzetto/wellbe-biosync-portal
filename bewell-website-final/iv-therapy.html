What is IV Nutrition Therapy?

IV Nutrition Therapy delivers essential vitamins, minerals, and nutrients directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for 100% absorption. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an IV therapy session take?

Most IV therapy sessions take 30-60 minutes, depending on the specific formulation and your individual needs. You can relax in our comfortable treatment rooms, read, work on your laptop, or simply rest while receiving your nutrients. The process is painless and many clients find it quite relaxing.

What IV formulations do you offer?

We offer customized IV formulations based on your specific health needs, including Myers' Cocktail for general wellness, high-dose vitamin C for immune support, NAD+ for cellular energy and anti-aging, glutathione for detoxification, and specialized blends for athletic performance, stress recovery, and beauty enhancement.

How often should I receive IV therapy?

The frequency depends on your health goals and current status. For general wellness, monthly sessions work well. For specific health conditions or during times of stress, weekly treatments may be beneficial. For performance enhancement or recovery, sessions may be scheduled around training or events. We'll create a personalized protocol based on your needs.
