What is Massage Therapy?

Massage therapy is a hands-on treatment that manipulates soft tissues, muscles, and tendons to promote healing, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being. Our licensed massage therapists use various techniques tailored to your specific needs and health goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first massage?

Your first session begins with a consultation to discuss your health history, areas of concern, and treatment goals. Your therapist will explain the techniques they'll use and ensure you're comfortable throughout the session. You'll be properly draped at all times for your privacy and comfort.

How often should I get a massage?

The frequency depends on your individual needs and goals. For general wellness, monthly sessions work well. For chronic pain or specific conditions, weekly or bi-weekly sessions may be recommended initially. Your therapist will create a personalized treatment plan based on your needs.

What types of massage do you offer?

We offer various massage techniques including Swedish, deep tissue, trigger point therapy, myofascial release, sports massage, and prenatal massage. Your therapist will recommend the best approach or combination of techniques based on your specific needs and preferences.
