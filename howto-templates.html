How to use Collect for payments and tip

Used for card tips and HSA/FSA payments
Open up the "Collect" app on the big ipad
Put in the amount that is getting charged
For the description
Patients first/last, service/reason for payment, staff they were treated by
FOR MANUAL CARD ENTRY
After amount and description put in - click grey continue button
Choose tip amount (if needed)
Click green "manual input" button
Add card numbers, expiration date, CVV and Zipcode and Click the blue "pay" button
Make sure to confirm the payment
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
FOR HANDHELD PAYMENT
After amount is entered click the three lines at the top right corner
It'll pull up a sidebar menu - look for "Connect Card Reader"
Click that, and click "Internet: WisePOS E, S700" to connect the handheld
Then go back to the home screen with the payment
Click the grey continue button
Payment will pop up on the handheld - add tip if needed
Take payment and make sure to click "confirm payment" button on ipad
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
IF PAYMENT IS FOR A TIP (massage or nurse)
In description make sure to write who the tip is for
Put in a $1 charge, and add the tip amount once the proper screen for tips pops up
Confirm total charge ($1.00 plus tip) and run payment
Take payment and make sure to click "confirm payment" button on ipad
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
AT THE END OF THE DAY: print out ALL collect charges/receipts and add to daily sheets folder

How to put a patient into WebPT

Make sure patient filled out the paperwork from IntakeQ
If the ID and insurance card were not uploaded, make sure to ask them for it and take a copy (keep for later)
Open WebPT from the favorited bookmarks
It should automatically let you login under PeytonSamons
If it gives you an error saying someone is already logged in, click "yes oust them"
Once the homepage loads in, look to the left and find where it says "patient manager"
Click add patient
In the "Patient Info" section:
Fill out the top with their prefix (Mr or Mrs), first and last name, birthday, and gender
In the "Addresses" section:
The "Addresses" tab should be open where you can add an address
Hit add address (it should be underlined)
Fill out address provided by the patient
In the Insurance section:
Add the insurance provided by the patient
Verify if insurance is through their job (PPO) or just regular health insurance
Add the subscriber (member) ID and the group number (should be provided on the insurance card)
Verify who the guarantor is - if they are not the guarantor, MAKE SURE TO GET THE GUARANTOR'S INFORMATION TO ADD INTO THE FILE (birthday, address, phone number)
In the Case section:
Click add case
Fill out each section that says REQUIRED
Title the case the abbreviation for whichever insurance they have and the current FULL date
BCBS - blue cross blue shield, UHC - united health care etc.. (XX/XX/XXXX)
If they have two insurances - find out which is primary and which is secondary
Should say "supplementary" on back of card for secondary - If still not sure - ask Silvio
Click the drop down arrow next to PRIMARY INSURANCE, the insurance you added should be the only option (if they have a secondary insurance, click the drop down arrow next to secondary and add it there)
Related Cause:
The related cause is always "None of the above" unless they are an Auto accident WITH A CLAIM
Make sure to specify if they were at fault of the accident or not
Make sure to get auto information if they are making a claim:
â€¢ Auto insurance they are using
â€¢ Claim number
â€¢ Attorney name and number
â€¢ Adjustor name and number
If they are an Auto case - add both their primary insurance and their Auto insurance
Label the case "Auto XX/XX/XXXX"
Make the auto insurance their secondary
Referring Physician:
The "Referring Physician" is always "Direct Access" unless the patient specifies otherwise
The doctor who referred them should be in the intake forms, if not ask the patient
The assigned therapist will always be Dr. Silvio Cozzetto
Click OK to save the case
In the "Contact Info" Tab:
Click add contact
The phone number option should pop up
MAKE SURE YOU CHANGE THE PHONE TYPE TO MOBILE
Click OK to save
Click add contact again
Change the contact type to EMAIL
Add patient's email and click OK to save
Scroll to the bottom and hit "Save and Verify Benefits"
A new screen will pop up
Leave the insurance as is
Change the discipline to "Pt & Chiro"
Hit "Use Group NPI" under therapist
Then hit Save and Verify Benefits

It should match with the case you just made with their insurance
Then hit save document
