How to Scan Daily Notes

In the back office, underneath the black left printer, there is a file cabinet
Open the top drawer, there should be a stack of daily chiro notes to scan
Each stack should have two sections separated by paperclips
The first section should be all of the daily sheets used that day (date on top right)
Remove the paperclip and set the other section (massage SOAPs aside)

How to Scan EOB's

Locate the stack of EOB's
Normally sitting on the scanner - it will look like a stack of mail
Locate the paper cutter (black square box in the compartments under the back office desktop)
Open all envelopes, keeping them in the same order as they originally were in
Once all envelopes are opened - take each EOB out and stack them, keeping the original order
Go through and remove all staples DOUBLE CHECK EACH PAGE - staples will break the scanner!
In sections, start scanning the stacks until you get through all of them - refer to the how to scan section
Once all EOB's have been scanned, hit finish scanning on the computer
Title the papers scanned the following:
BeWell EOB's
XX/XX/XXXX (current date)
Now go back through all the EOB's
Detach all of the checks then *go back and double check* that all checks have been taken out of the rest of the EOB's
Paperclip the checks together, leave them on top of the scanned EOB's, wait for Silvio's instruction

How to use Collect for payments and tip

Used for card tips and HSA/FSA payments
Open up the "Collect" app on the big ipad
Put in the amount that is getting charged
For the description
Patients first/last, service/reason for payment, staff they were treated by
FOR MANUAL CARD ENTRY
After amount and description put in - click grey continue button
Choose tip amount (if needed)
Click green "manual input" button
Add card numbers, expiration date, CVV and Zipcode and Click the blue "pay" button
Make sure to confirm the payment
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
FOR HANDHELD PAYMENT
After amount is entered click the three lines at the top right corner
It'll pull up a sidebar menu - look for "Connect Card Reader"
Click that, and click "Internet: WisePOS E, S700" to connect the handheld
Then go back to the home screen with the payment
Click the grey continue button
Payment will pop up on the handheld - add tip if needed
Take payment and make sure to click "confirm payment" button on ipad
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
IF PAYMENT IS FOR A TIP (massage or nurse)
In description make sure to write who the tip is for
Put in a $1 charge, and add the tip amount once the proper screen for tips pops up
Confirm total charge ($1.00 plus tip) and run payment
Take payment and make sure to click "confirm payment" button on ipad
If customer wants receipt: click "email receipt" and put address in
AT THE END OF THE DAY: print out ALL collect charges/receipts and add to daily sheets folder
