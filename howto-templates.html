How to Locate and Turn on the Scanner

In the back office, next to the desktop, there is a black scanner
Open the paperholder at the bottom, and press scan to turn it on (the whole block next to "scan" should light up blue)
Turn on the desktop - passkey is 6570
Once you start scanning, it will automatically open the drop box to upload to

How to Scan

Make sure the blue button is lit up next to "scan"
Make sure all papers are aligned
Stacks should be no thicker than the size of your pinky - Anything more will jam the scanner and now you're in troubleâ€¦
MAKE SURE THERE ARE NO STAPLES
Place the stack of papers FACING AWAY, right side up in the top portion of scanner - Hit scan
Once scanned through, a pop up will come onto the computer screen asking if you would like to continue scanning or if you are finished scanning - If you have more that stack that did not fit in the initial scan, click continue scanning and finish the stack, repeat until stack is fully scanned
Once all papers are scanned, hit finish scanning
Another screen will pop up
The title will be highlighted blue, delete the automatic title and add in the new one
Hit save and it should automatically open and save to the dropbox

How to Scan Daily Notes

In the back office, underneath the black left printer, there is a file cabinet
Open the top drawer, there should be a stack of daily chiro notes to scan
Each stack should have two sections separated by paperclips
The first section should be all of the daily sheets used that day (date on top right)
Remove the paperclip and set the other section (massage SOAPs aside)
Place this section on the black scanner in back (refer to the how to scan section)
Once scanned through, title it "BHAM CHIRO daily" and the date (XX/XX/XXXX)
Hit save, and it will automatically save it to the drop box - You can now put this section on the shredding machine
Massage SOAP Notes Section:
- Remove paperclip from second section
- Place massage SOAP notes on black scanner in back
- Scan through documents
- Title scan: "MASSAGE SOAP notes" and current date (XX/XX/XXXX)
- Save to dropbox
- Shred original documents

How to Scan EOB's

Process:
- Locate stack of EOBs (usually on scanner)
- Use paper cutter to open envelopes
- Keep documents in original order
- Remove ALL staples (double-check)
- Scan in sections
- Title scan: "BeWell EOB's XX/XX/XXXX"
- Detach checks, double-check all checks removed
- Paperclip checks together
- Wait for Silvio's instructions
