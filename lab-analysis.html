Frequently Asked Questions

How is your lab analysis different from regular blood work?

Our comprehensive analysis goes far beyond standard blood panels. We test for optimal ranges (not just normal ranges), include functional medicine markers, assess nutrient status at the cellular level, and look for patterns that reveal root causes of symptoms. We also provide detailed interpretation and personalized recommendations based on your results.

How should I prepare for lab testing?

Preparation depends on the specific tests ordered. Generally, we recommend fasting for 12-14 hours for comprehensive panels, avoiding alcohol for 24 hours, staying hydrated, and continuing medications unless specifically instructed otherwise. We'll provide detailed preparation instructions when you schedule your appointment.

When will I get my results?

Most results are available within 3-7 business days, though some specialized tests may take up to 2 weeks. We'll schedule a follow-up consultation to review your results in detail, explain what they mean for your health, and discuss personalized treatment recommendations based on your findings.
