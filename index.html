Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I know which service is right for me?
We recommend starting with our BioSync assessment for just $50. This intelligent tool analyzes your symptoms and health goals to recommend the most appropriate treatment plan. Alternatively, you can schedule a consultation where our experts will create a personalized wellness plan based on your specific needs.
Do I need a referral to visit Be Well?
No referral is needed! We accept self-referrals and welcome anyone seeking to improve their health and wellness. Simply book your appointment online or call us directly.
What should I expect during my first visit?
Your first visit includes a comprehensive health assessment, discussion of your wellness goals, and creation of a personalized treatment plan. We'll review your medical history, perform any necessary examinations, and explain all recommended treatments. Plan for about 60-90 minutes for your initial consultation.

Services & Treatments

What is the Total Transformation program?
Our Total Transformation program is a comprehensive wellness journey that addresses every aspect of your health. It includes personalized nutrition planning, targeted treatments (chiropractic, IV therapy, etc.), fitness guidance, stress management, and ongoing support. The program typically runs 12-16 weeks and is customized to your specific goals, whether that's weight loss, pain relief, energy optimization, or complete lifestyle change.
How often will I need treatments?
Treatment frequency varies based on your condition and goals. Initially, you may need 2-3 visits per week, gradually decreasing as you improve. Some services like IV therapy might be weekly or monthly, while others like chiropractic care may start more frequently then transition to maintenance visits. We'll create a schedule that fits your needs and lifestyle.
Are your treatments safe?
Absolutely. All our treatments are performed by licensed, experienced practitioners using FDA-approved equipment and protocols. We thoroughly review your medical history and current medications to ensure all treatments are safe for you. Our integrated approach means all your providers communicate to ensure coordinated, safe care.

Insurance & Payment

Do you accept insurance?
We accept many major insurance plans for covered services like chiropractic care and physiotherapy. However, some services like IV therapy and peptides may not be covered. We'll verify your benefits before treatment and provide transparent pricing for all services. We also offer flexible payment plans and package discounts.
What payment options do you offer?
We accept cash, all major credit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and offer financing through CareCredit. We also have membership programs and package deals that provide significant savings for multiple treatments. Our team will work with you to find a payment solution that fits your budget.
Can I use my HSA or FSA?
Yes! Most of our services qualify for HSA/FSA payment, including chiropractic care, physiotherapy, lab testing, and many other treatments. We can provide detailed receipts with appropriate coding for reimbursement or direct payment.

Results & Expectations

How quickly will I see results?
Results vary by individual and treatment type. Some patients experience immediate relief after chiropractic adjustments or IV therapy, while others may need several sessions. Typically, you'll notice improvements within 2-4 weeks of consistent treatment. Our Total Transformation program shows significant results within 4-6 weeks, with full transformation achieved in 12-16 weeks.
What if the treatment doesn't work for me?
We're committed to your success. If you're not seeing expected results, we'll reassess your treatment plan and make adjustments. Our integrated approach means we have many tools available - if one treatment isn't optimal, we can try complementary approaches. We also offer satisfaction guarantees on certain programs.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Absolutely! We believe in long-term wellness, not quick fixes. After your initial treatment phase, we offer maintenance programs, regular check-ins, and ongoing support. Many patients join our wellness membership for continued access to treatments and resources at discounted rates.
