What is Colon Hydrotherapy?

Colon hydrotherapy, also known as colonic irrigation, is a safe and gentle procedure that cleanses the colon using warm, filtered water. This natural detoxification process helps remove accumulated waste, toxins, and gas, promoting better digestive health and overall well-being. Our certified therapists use state-of-the-art, FDA-approved equipment to ensure your comfort and safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is colon hydrotherapy safe?

Yes, when performed by certified professionals using FDA-approved equipment, colon hydrotherapy is very safe. We use sterile, disposable tubing for each session and maintain the highest hygiene standards. Our therapists are I-ACT certified and trained in proper techniques and safety protocols.

What should I expect during a session?

A typical session lasts 45-60 minutes in a private, comfortable room. You'll lie on your side while a small, sterile speculum is gently inserted. Warm, filtered water flows in and waste flows out through a closed system. Your therapist will monitor the process and may perform gentle abdominal massage to help release trapped gas and waste.

How often should I have colon hydrotherapy?

The frequency depends on your individual health goals and condition. Some people benefit from a series of 3-6 sessions initially, followed by maintenance sessions monthly or seasonally. Others may need more frequent sessions for specific health conditions. Your therapist will create a personalized plan based on your needs.
